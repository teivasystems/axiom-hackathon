## Director's Notes

**Alex** — the opener sets the tone. No introduction, no "Hi, I'm...". Jump straight to the line. The abruptness is intentional.

**Clips 1–5** — same rhythm, same pace. Build the drumbeat. Judges should start to sense the pattern by clip 3.

**Riley (clip 6)** — the slight tonal shift ("*And* I'm telling you about it") signals awareness. Riley knows this is a pitch. It's the only clip with a hint of meta-humour before Kostya.

**Kostya (clip 7)** — the punchline. Pause before the line. Let the silence sit for 1.5 seconds after the previous 6 AIs have each claimed authorship. Then: "I pressed the buttons." This should get a laugh. Do not rush it. Do not smile. Deadpan is everything.

**After Kostya's line** — brief beat, then cut to the problem statement or a title card. Do not let the sequence drag.
